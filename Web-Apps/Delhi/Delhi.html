Delhi Bank analysis

Introduction

With the help of 'Delhi ATM buffer analysis', the management section of any bank can get basic idea related to transaction happened using their bank's ATM respectively. Which will clearly help the organization to perform analysis and will give idea regarding cash refill time and establishment of new ATM machines in the areas which have higher requirement according to survey.

This module is devided in two parts.
1> Normal Users
2> Administration
Normal users can get the bank name as well as the ATM name. Where as in admin section bank adminisration can get the information about the tra done in each atm as well as the amount available in atm at the start of the day.
